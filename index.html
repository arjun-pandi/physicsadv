Guidelines
Here are some rules for a WhatsApp community to share information:
1. Be kind and inclusive. Treat everyone with respect.
2. No hate speech or bullying. Degrading comments and personal attacks aren’t acceptable.
3. No promotions or spam. Self-promotion and irrelevant messages aren’t allowed.
4. Communicate with known contacts. Only send messages to those who have contacted you first or have requested you contact them on WhatsApp.
5. Ask for permission and respect boundaries. You should get permission from contacts before you add them to a group.
6. Use group controls. Admin only message setting for WhatsApp groups is available
